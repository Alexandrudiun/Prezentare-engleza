Explore Romania

Quick Facts

Romania is a country located in southeastern Europe, known for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. Here are some quick facts about Romania:

  • Capital: Bucharest
  • Population: 19.5 million
  • Official language: Romanian
  • Currency: Romanian leu (RON)
  • Time zone: Eastern European Time (EET)
  • Main religions: Eastern Orthodox, Protestant, Roman Catholic

Top Attractions

If you're planning a trip to Romania, here are some of the top attractions to check out:

  • Bran Castle - a Gothic castle associated with the legend of Dracula
  • Palace of the Parliament - a massive government building in Bucharest, the second largest administrative building in the world after the Pentagon
  • Painted Monasteries of Bucovina - a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for their unique painted exterior walls
  • Transfagarasan Highway - a scenic mountain road that crosses the Carpathian Mountains
  • Danube Delta - a UNESCO biosphere reserve and one of Europe's largest wetlands, home to over 300 species of birds
